Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I purchase movie tickets online?

Visit our website, choose a cinema, select your desired movie and time, then proceed to checkout.

2. Is advance booking available?

Yes, you can book tickets up to 3 days in advance. Early booking is recommended for popular movies.

3. Do I need an account to avail advanced booking?

Yes, creating an account is required to complete avail advanced booking.

4. What cinema locations do you operate in?

We are located in Manila (Rizal Avenue), Quezon City (Quezon Avenue), and Makati (Greenbelt 3).

5. Are cashless payments accepted?

Yes, we accept GCash, Maya, and credit/debit cards.

6. Can I choose my seat during booking?

Yes, our system lets you select available seats using a seating chart.

7. How will I receive my tickets?

After successful payment, your e-ticket will be sent via email.

8. Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Bookings are non-refundable. However, you may contact our support for rescheduling, depending on availability.
